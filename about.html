"Largest Music Festival in Africa"

Mawazine Music
Festival
2022

The Mawazine Music Festival is the largest conference dedicated to music in the Africa. Held every year in Rabat Morroco, the conference features speakers from the music industry, musicians, and music education professionals. This year’s conference will be held on July 25-27 at the same place.

Mawazine Music Festifal

The logo for the Mawazine Music Festival 2022 was decided through the logo competition from 21st to 22nd of July this year. After online public voting and Selection Committee's judging process,the logo from Maaloum Ely Cheick won the price.
